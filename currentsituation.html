The Current Situation with AI

So, currently, artificial intelligence is being used in online customer service on websites and also being used for public "services" in forms of ChatGPT and other AIs (GPT is the most common).
The whole deal with this is to help people with gathering information. It's widely used already, by around 1/4 of Americans (already!) and it's still growing.
It seems harmless for now, but there could be issues in the future.
