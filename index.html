The Increasing Usage of AI

The invention of artificial intelligence has brought so many opportunities to humanity. However, people are slowly getting their jobs taken away from this.

AI is used for many purposes - regardless if you can tell if it is or not. Even our teachers throughout the school will use AI sometimes.
It's such a useful invention and it's being used greatly, however AI is being used in order to completely replace humans, and people don't realize this.

Eventually, you'll get a job, and you might even be replaced too. It sounds crazy, but that's just the world we are inventing.
