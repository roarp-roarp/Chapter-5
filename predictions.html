The Predictions of AI

Artificial intelligence could eventually overtake human actions. Jobs would be completely removed by robots being cheaper over time to run, or "pay", and also being a lot easier to manage.

Jobs like online support are already getting replaced by technology, and some are going to probably be replaced in 10-20 years, like cashiers and maybe even mechanic depending on how
things go. I personally believe that it won't happen for another 30-50 years, but that's because I believe that everything needs to be refined and made sure that it works perfectly with no bugs.

Robots are very easy to manipulate and hack; and this could lead to many, many problems if bad people figure out how to hack or shut down robots and stores that are this important.
